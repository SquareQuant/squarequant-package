## How to Contribute

### Reporting Bugs
- **Open an issue** on [GitHub](https://github.com/SquareQuant/squarequant-package/issues) with a clear, concise description of the bug.
- **Include**:
  - Steps to reproduce the issue.
  - Expected vs. actual behavior.
  - Your environment (Python version, OS, and any relevant dependencies).
- **Label your issue** as `bug` for better tracking.

### Suggesting Features
- **Open an issue** on [GitHub](https://github.com/SquareQuant/squarequant-package/issues) with a detailed description of your proposed feature.
- **Explain**:
  - The use case and why it would benefit the community.
  - Any relevant examples or references (e.g., similar features in other tools).
- **Label your issue** as `enhancement`.
